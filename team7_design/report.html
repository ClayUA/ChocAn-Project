Paragraph

For this project we had to address the issues of coupling and cohesion throughout our diagrams. Throughout most of the diagram we used cohesion to solve the majority of our problems. For example, report accesses: member report, provider report, summary report, and EFT report. All of those classes are not accessed by any other class in the Class. We performed similar cohesion for Main Menu and Record. We also used coupling so solve a few of our issues regarding User. We used tight coupling for User, Card, Record, and Report by letting them all access, Manager, Operator, and Provider. Our ChocAn Computer also is an example of coupling because it's used for Card, Terminal, and Timer. One main reason why our solution to this problem is good is that it not only addresses the issue of classes interacting with each other, it is done in an organized and efficient manner.
